STRESS MANAGEMENT

the following self management practices
(1) plan for self care atleast 20 mins to
as much as 1 hour per day . This can
mean performing exercise , getting a massage , 
or reading a book . A book should be
non-business book . 
(2) Connect with a friend on phone or 
video conferencing . 
(3) Take a Yoga class . Take time for a 
long walk or hike . 
(4) Simply give a space for your self .
